# 💡 Project Title

**Vernacular AI Tutor: Learn from Your Own Study Material**

---

# 🧠 Core Idea (Simple Explanation)

> An AI tutor that takes a PDF, asks questions from it, identifies what the learner doesn’t understand, and reteaches those concepts in their native language (like Tamil).

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

* Students study from PDFs but don’t know:

  * what they actually understood ❌
  * where they are weak ❌
* No personalized feedback
* Most AI tools are:

  * English-only
  * Not adaptive

👉 Result: **inefficient learning**

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

Build a system that:

1. Takes any **PDF (notes, textbook, etc.)**
2. Converts it into an **interactive quiz**
3. Evaluates user answers
4. Detects weak areas
5. Teaches only those parts in **simple vernacular language**
6. Re-tests the user

👉 Basically: **PDF → Personal Tutor**

---

# 🔁 Workflow (Step-by-Step)

```text id="workflow1"
Upload PDF
   ↓
Extract text
   ↓
Split into chunks (topics)
   ↓
Store in vector DB (RAG)
   ↓
Generate questions (LLM)
   ↓
User answers
   ↓
Evaluate answers
   ↓
Detect weak concepts
   ↓
Retrieve relevant content (RAG)
   ↓
Explain in Tamil (LLM)
   ↓
Ask follow-up question
```
